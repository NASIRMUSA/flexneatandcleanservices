CLEANING REMINDER

All cleaning reminders must be acknowledged. Failure to do so means the cleaning will still go on as booked and planned, and the associate would be sent. If the associate is not allowed in and the cleaning needs to be rescheduled, a 30% penalty fee will be collected.
